# Hello World

Hey there, welcome to my blog. I hope to fill this site with a variety of things, from development and coding to short fiction. I've been working on an idea for this for a long time, with a site that I previously developed many years ago, before I knew more about development and content management (not that I know all that much more now).

I've been working in tech for over 5 years now, recently getting a position as an information systems specialist for our local school district. In that time, I have learned so much about developing in many languages and for a variety of purposes.

However, I also have a love for fiction and want to stretch my imaginative side of the brain, as all I use is the analytical side professionally. I also hope to get some humor out. I like to think I'm funny (my wife would disagree).

So with all that said, I have some categories that I want to explore:

**Nonsense**. This will be just about anything I want to post in general. Random updates, dumb musings, and anything that doesn't fit into the other categories.

**Developing in Public**. These posts will be about development, usually about my journey and things I've learned. I hope to make some posts about progress on ideas I have or on things I've learned.

**Fiction**. These are things I made up. I'm a fan of fiction, having a novel in progress at all times. I really hope to explore of creativity along the way and flex some brain muscles that have been dormant for a while.

**A Word**. I will explore odd or unused words and create a post themed around that idea. This will also help my brain through creativity and things about a wide variety of things.

So to the zero people reading this, welcome.
